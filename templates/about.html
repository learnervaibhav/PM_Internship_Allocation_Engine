About Us

The PM Internship Scheme is designed to connect students with internship opportunities in IT and allied sectors. This AI-powered allocation system ensures fair and transparent distribution of internships based on eligibility, skills, and priority rules.

Features include:

  • AI-driven resume parsing and skill extraction
  • Multi-layered filtering (age, income, experience, category)
  • Ranking based on skills and education
  • Live performance monitoring and dashboards
